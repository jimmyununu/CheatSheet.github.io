The Box Model

What is the box model?

The box model is essentially a box that wraps around every HTML element

The box model Consist of four parts

1.) Margin

Clears an area outside the border. Margin is transparent.

My margin's are set to 10
So are mine! We are 20 pixels apart.

2.) Border

A border that goes around the padding and content

I have a dotted border!

3.) Padding

Clears an area around the content. The padding is transparent

I have a little padding.
I have a lot of padding!
By default the padding either grows the elemnt's size or shrinks the content itself.

4.) Content

The content of the box, where text and images appear.
